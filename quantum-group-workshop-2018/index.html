Ohad's Research - Quantum Group Workshop 2018

Quantum Group Workshop 2018

  • When: 10am-5pm, Tuesday, 19th June 2018
  • Where: Lecture Theatre B, Wolfson Building, University of Oxford Department of Computer Science

Schedule

• 10:00 Arrival
• 10:25 Welcome
• 10:30 Session I: Causality
• Quantum shannon theory with indefinite causal order / Giulio Chiribella
• Bounds on correlations with indefinite causal orders / Subhayan Roy Moulik
• Quantum causal models / Robin Lorenz
• TBA / Jon Barrett
• The sources of causal cognition / Selma Coecke

• 11:20 Short break

• 11:30 Session II: Quantum information theory
• Contextuality that survives unknown measurements / Matthew Pusey
• A categorical reconstruction of quantum theory / Sean Tull
• A fundamental theorem for CQM? / Sam Staton

• 12:00 Lunch (independently)

• 13:30 Session III: Diagrams
• Quantum diagrams for kids / Bob Coecke
• Doing linear algebra stuff in the ZX-calculus / Quanlong Wang
• ZW-calculus, a calculus with two braidings / Kang Feng Ng
• Conjecture synthesis / Hector Miller Bakewell

• 14:10 Coffee

• 14:50 Session IV: Medley
• TBA / Stefano Gogioso
• Approximate error correction via inverse quantum information theory / Niel de Beaudrap
• High-level methods for homotopy construction in a model of infinity categories / Jamie Vicary

• 15:30 Short break

• 15:40 Session V: Probability
• Probability for open games / Joe Bolt
• A language for constructing smooth probability densities / Swaraj Dash
• No-go theorems for monad compositions / Maaike Zwart
• A domain theory for quasi-Borel spaces / Ohad Kammar

• 18:00 BBQ and other shenanigans
